GRE Verbal Practice

Use the GREPrep Verbal Practice to help you prepare for the computer-delivered GRE General Test. The Verbal Practice Quizzes are designed to help you:
  • Understand what's being tested Gain familiarity with the various question types and difficulty level of Verbal type of questions
  • Practice your test-taking strategies by understanding the topics thoroughly
  • Use the concepts you have prepared in verbal question types

Instructions
  • The Quiz is untimed i.e. you will have unlimited time to complete it. You can choose the number of questions to attempt before taking the quiz.
  • The questions consist of Verbal types.
  • The questions can either be multiple choice or single choice types
  • Once you submit your test, you can get your quiz score. Each question will be of 1 mark.
  • You can use buttons like First, Previous, Next, and Last to move to questions
  • Select the type of verbal questions you want to attempt
  • Select the number of questions you want to attempt
  • Click on Start Your Quiz button below to start your Quiz.
  • Once you click on the button, the test starts immediately.
